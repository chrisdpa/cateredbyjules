Private Party Catering

Allow Catered by Jules to come into your home and create a memorable dining experience. We hear that your place is the place 'to be'! From summer garden buffets to winter dinner parties there are many occasions where we can do all the hard work for you. You set the ambience, the music and we take care of the rest. We will even do the washing up so you don't have to - perfect!

Bespoke Baking

Everyone loves a home baked treat. Catered by Jules would like to be your 'go to' baker. Ranging from home delivered cakes and treats to bespoke wedding cake creations we can deliver that special something to you .We also know that sometimes mid week you just need a pick me up so why not get in touch and ask about our cake deliveries to your office and keep your staff smiling.

Chef for Hire

Wherever you may be, talk to us about hiring a chef for that personal touch. In your home, holiday home or even campsite Catered by Jules will come to you and create anything from a low key supper for friends to a glamorous canapé reception. We will even do all the washing up - what could be better?

Corporate Catering

Catered by Jules can cater for a variety of venues and events across London. Getting to know our clients is an integral part of being able to provide you with a bespoke service. From canapé parties, cooking demonstrations to corporate parties allow us to manage all the details to guarantee a fantastic experience.
